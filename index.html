About Me

I'm Harris Joseph, aspiring data scientist and developer, mathematician, mechanic, outdoorsman, and friend to many! After working as a professional automotive technician for a few years in my early twenties, I decided to go back to school and get a bachelor's degree. Four years later, in May of 2017, I finished my bachelor's in applied mathematics, and here I am! I am passionate about finding creative solutions to unique problems, and I am constantly working to branch my skill tree. I hope you enjoy yourself, if not, feel free to contact me and chip my ego. This website may have started as a form of shameless self promotion, but it is constantly evolving with its creator, yours truly.
